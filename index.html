Day-by-Day Plans

Sample Itineraries from Chennai

All itineraries begin at Chennai. Travel time is included in Day 1 unless specified.

Ooty – Nilgiri Breeze (3N / 4D)

DEPARTS FRI 8 PM
Day 1
Chennai → Ooty (Overnight Journey)
Depart Chennai at 8:00 PM from Koyambedu CMBT. Board AC sleeper bus. Scenic drive through Mettupalayam, arrive Ooty by 6:00 AM.
Day 2
Ooty Sightseeing – Hills & Gardens
Check-in, freshen up. Visit Botanical Garden, Rose Garden, Doddabetta Peak (highest in Nilgiris at 2,637 m). Evening: Ooty Lake boating, local market.
Botanical Garden Doddabetta Peak Rose Garden Ooty Lake
Day 3
Nilgiri Railway & Tea Country
Morning: UNESCO Nilgiri Mountain Railway heritage ride (Ooty to Coonoor). Afternoon: Tea factory visit, Sim's Park Coonoor, Lamb's Rock viewpoint. Return to Ooty.
Nilgiri Mountain Railway Tea Factory Sim's Park Lamb's Rock
Day 4
Avalanche Lake → Return to Chennai
Morning: Visit Avalanche Lake, Emerald Lake. Check-out by 12 PM. Depart Ooty, arrive Chennai by 10:00–11:00 PM.
Avalanche Lake Emerald Lake

Pondicherry – Pondy Getaway (1N / 2D)

DEPARTS SUN 6 AM
Day 1
Chennai → Pondicherry → Beach & French Quarter
Depart Chennai at 6:00 AM, arrive Pondicherry by 9:30 AM (via ECR). Check-in. Visit Promenade Beach, Rock Beach, Goubert Market. Afternoon: French Quarter walk — Rue de la Marine, Rue Suffren, cafés. Evening: Sunset at Promenade.
Promenade Beach Rock Beach French Quarter Goubert Market
Day 2
Auroville, Paradise Beach → Return Chennai
Early morning: Sri Aurobindo Ashram. Mid-morning: Auroville and Matrimandir (outer viewing). Afternoon: Paradise Beach via Chunnambar boat ride. Depart by 5 PM, arrive Chennai by 9 PM.
Sri Aurobindo Ashram Auroville Matrimandir Paradise Beach

Munnar – Kerala Mist (4N / 5D)

DEPARTS THU 7 PM
Day 1
Chennai → Coimbatore → Munnar
Depart Chennai 7 PM. Overnight journey to Coimbatore. Morning transfer to Munnar via Adimali. Check-in by afternoon. Evening at leisure.
Day 2
Eravikulam & Tea Estates
Visit Eravikulam National Park (Nilgiri Tahr habitat). Tea Museum, Rajamala Hills. Afternoon: Attukal Waterfalls. Evening: local market walk.
Eravikulam NP Tea Museum Rajamala Hills Attukal Falls
Day 3
Mattupetty & Kundala
Mattupetty Dam & Indo-Swiss Livestock Farm. Echo Point. Kundala Lake (shikara boating). Top Station (highest motorable point in Munnar, 1,700m).
Mattupetty Dam Echo Point Kundala Lake Top Station
Day 4
Valara & Cheeyappara Waterfalls
Morning excursion to Valara and Cheeyappara Waterfalls en route to Ernakulam. Optional: Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary visit (extra cost). Evening: shopping at Munnar town.
Day 5
Munnar → Chennai (Return)
Check-out by 9 AM. Depart for Coimbatore. Board return bus/train to Chennai. Arrive by 10 PM–11 PM.

Rameswaram – Pilgrimage Special (2N / 3D)

DEPARTS TUE 6 PM
Day 1
Chennai → Rameswaram
Depart Chennai at 6 PM. Overnight bus journey. Arrive Rameswaram by 5 AM. Check-in. Early morning holy dip at Agni Theertham beach.
Day 2
Temple Darshan & Dhanushkodi
Morning: Ramanathaswamy Temple — 22 sacred theertham baths (corridor is the longest in Asia at 1.2 km). Afternoon: Dhanushkodi ghost town, tip of India land's end. Pamban Bridge viewing. Evening: Kothandaramaswamy Temple.
Ramanathaswamy Temple Dhanushkodi Pamban Bridge Kothandaramaswamy Temple
Day 3
Mandapam & Return to Chennai
Morning: Gandhamadhana Parvatham (footprint of Rama). Marine National Park, Mandapam. Depart by noon. Arrive Chennai by 11 PM.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

What packages are available for Ooty from Chennai?
We offer the "Nilgiri Breeze" package — 3 Nights / 4 Days at ₹8,500 per person. It departs every Friday at 8 PM from Koyambedu CMBT and includes AC transport, 3-star hotel, breakfast & dinner, Nilgiri Mountain Railway ride, and guided sightseeing to Botanical Garden, Doddabetta Peak, Avalanche Lake, and Coonoor Tea Estates.
How many days is the Munnar trip from Chennai?
The Munnar "Kerala Mist" package is 4 Nights / 5 Days. We depart from Chennai at 7 PM on Day 1 and return by Day 5 evening (around 10–11 PM). Highlights include Eravikulam National Park, Mattupetty Dam, Kundala Lake, Tea Museum, and Top Station. Price is ₹12,000 per person for groups of 15 or more.
What is the Pondicherry itinerary?
Day 1: Depart Chennai at 6 AM, arrive by 9:30 AM. Visit Promenade Beach, Rock Beach, French Quarter. Evening sunset at Promenade. Day 2: Sri Aurobindo Ashram, Auroville, Paradise Beach via Chunnambar boat ride. Return to Chennai by 9 PM. Price: ₹4,500 per person.

Our most affordable package is the "Pondy Getaway" to Pondicherry — 1 Night / 2 Days at ₹4,500 per person. It departs every Sunday and includes AC transport, hotel, breakfast & dinner, and sightseeing to Auroville and Paradise Beach.
Is there a group discount?
Yes. Groups of 10–20 get 5% off, 21–40 get 10% off, and 41+ travellers get 15% off the standard per-person price. Discounts apply to the base package price and cannot be combined with other offers.
What is the cancellation policy?
30 or more days before travel: full refund. 15–29 days before travel: 50% refund. Less than 15 days: no refund. You can reschedule once for free if done at least 20 days before the trip. Reschedule requests within 20 days incur a ₹500 per person fee.

You can book via WhatsApp at +91 98400 00000, call the same number during office hours (Mon–Sat, 9 AM – 7 PM), or visit our office in T. Nagar, Chennai. A 30% advance payment is required to confirm your booking. Balance is due 7 days before departure.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), NEFT/IMPS bank transfer, and cash at our T. Nagar office. EMI options are available for packages priced above ₹25,000 per person through select banks.
Do you have packages for international destinations?
Currently, SunRoute Travels specialises in domestic Indian destinations. For international travel enquiries, please contact us on WhatsApp and our team will connect you with a partner agency that handles international tours.
Are the packages suitable for senior citizens?
Yes. Pondicherry, Rameswaram, and Ooty are particularly well-suited for senior travellers. We can arrange ground-floor rooms, vegetarian meals, a slower-paced sightseeing schedule, and private transport on request. Mention senior-friendly requirements when you book.
What is the best time to visit Ooty / Kodaikanal / Munnar?
Ooty and Kodaikanal are best from March to June (cool, pleasant, flowers in bloom) and September to November (post-monsoon greenery). Munnar is best from September to March. We recommend avoiding the peak monsoon months (July–August) for hill stations as roads can be difficult. Pondicherry and Rameswaram are suitable year-round.
