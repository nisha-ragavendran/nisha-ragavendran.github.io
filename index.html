why kelvi

Built around the one thing customers always bring you: a question.

In Tamil, Kelvi means “question” — கேள்வி. That meaning is at the heart of the brand: every ticket, every ping, every call starts with a question. Kelvi answers those questions instantly, consistently, and at scale.

A name with meaning

Kelvi bridges language, intent, and action — turning incoming questions into guided answers, automated resolutions, and clean handoffs when a human is needed.

support channels

AI Chat Widgets

Fast answers on your website and app

Drop in a widget with a few lines of code. Kelvi learns from your knowledge base and resolves common questions before they reach your team.

WhatsApp Agents

Conversational support where customers respond fastest

Automate order updates, FAQs, reminders, and support flows with a WhatsApp-first experience that feels immediate and personal.

Email Agents

Triage, draft, and resolve incoming mail

Voice Call Agents

Pick up, understand, and route calls

Handle common call intents with AI voice agents and send complex cases to the right human with full context intact.
